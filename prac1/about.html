About Us

We are a small family-run local business, located and operating in Cairville for 5 years.

We have,however, over 25 years of experience in floristry.

We are very familiar with the types and language of flowers, and we can select the most suitable flowers for you as gifts according to your needs.

Red Roses
When you want to give your partner a love surprise, red roses must be your choice.
Carnations
When you want to give a gift to your hardworking mother, carnations will express your deepest love for her.
